# PERFORMANCE WORK STATEMENT (PWS)
## Enterprise IT Service Desk Support Services
### (Fictional sample for testing — not a real contract)

**Contractor:** Meridian Federal Solutions, LLC
**Customer:** U.S. Department of Commerce
**Contract Type:** Firm-Fixed-Price
**Period of Performance:** Base year + four option years

## 1. Scope
The Contractor shall provide a fully managed Enterprise IT Service Desk supporting
approximately 9,200 users across 14 geographic sites. Services shall be delivered
using the ServiceNow ITSM platform and shall conform to ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018.

## 2. Required Services
- Tier 1 and Tier 2 incident management (24x7x365)
- Service request fulfillment via a published service catalogue
- Problem management and root-cause analysis
- Change enablement with a Change Advisory Board (CAB)
- Configuration and asset management (CMDB)
- Knowledge management and self-service portal
- Service level management and monthly reporting

## 3. Service Levels (SLAs)
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Priority 1 (Critical) resolution | 4 hours, 95% |
| Priority 2 resolution | 8 business hours, 90% |
| First Contact Resolution | >= 70% |
| Service Desk availability | 99.5% |
| Average speed of answer | <= 30 seconds |
| Customer satisfaction | >= 90% |

## 4. Standards & Compliance
The Contractor shall establish, operate, and maintain a Service Management System (SMS)
conforming to ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 and shall achieve certification within 12 months of
contract award. The Contractor shall comply with applicable federal security requirements
including FISMA and NIST SP 800-53.

## 5. Reporting
The Contractor shall deliver a monthly performance report by the 5th business day of each
month, covering SLA attainment, ticket volumes, trends, and continual improvement actions.

## 6. Governance
Monthly service reviews with the customer and quarterly management reviews shall be held.
